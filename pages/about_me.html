    Who am I?

    Hello there, I'm bsod, a self-taught programmer from Earth with a passion for open source. I started learning Python as a hobby to develop a Discord bot, and quickly fell in love with programming.

    Currently, I'm pursuing a bachelors in electronics. In my free time, I enjoy making music on FL Studio.

    While I'm not sure what type of programmer I am by nature, I can tell you that front-end development is not my forte. Nonetheless, I'm always eager to learn and expand my skills, and I'm excited to see where my programming journey takes me next.

    Quick Bit

    It all started when I was in 12th grade during the COVID pandemic. One of my classmates was developing a Discord bot and I was impressed by it. So, I decided to learn how to make one myself. Over time, I kept working on it and adding new features.

    Even though I was studying biology at the time, I got a lot of attention from my computer science classmates because I would help them out. I still work on the bot today and continue to improve it.
