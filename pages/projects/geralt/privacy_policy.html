    At Geralt, I take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and disclose information that I receive from users of my Discord bot, Geralt. By using Geralt, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

    What I Collect?

    I collect information that is necessary for Geralt to function properly. This includes:

    -- User IDs: I collect user IDs to enable Geralt to identify individual users.
    -- Channel IDs: I collect channel IDs to enable Geralt to perform actions in the correct channels.
    -- Guild IDs: I collect guild IDs to enable Geralt to perform actions in the correct server.

    In addition, I may collect other information such as server configuration data, message content, bot usage statistics, timestamps of when certain actions occur. This information is used solely for the purpose of improving, enhancing, and for the proper functioning of Geralt.

    How I use this Information

    I use the information collected to operate, maintain, and improve Geralt, and to provide you with a personalized experience. Specifically, I use your information to:

  • Provide you with personalized content and experiences
  • Perform analytics and data analysis to improve Geralt
  • Develop and improve the quality of Geralt and my services
  • Respond to user inquiries and requests
