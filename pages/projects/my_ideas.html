    Future Ideas

    I am a visionary, with an infinite wellspring of creative inspiration 💀. Just joking, as of now I only have three major ideas when it comes to projects which are purely based on software. The following explain what they are in detail:

    Credential Manager

    The idea of creating a simple credential manager popped up in my head as soon as I was fluent with OOP in Python. Its a simple class which creates an instance for a user to sign up to the application and store his/her credentials. However, the entire application built in Python was CLI based. To make this GUI, I got to know that C# was the best language for Windows applications.

    Click here for credential_manager.py and here for the sample config.env utilised for starting up the entire application. FYI, it doesn't have all the features. Just the bare bones for now.

    However, I recently learnt that does the same thing and is also open source 🗿. I was also planning on making mine open source and lmao, BitWarden is also built on C#. But screw them, I'm still going to make this application soon lol.

    Grt Price Scrapper

    My father is keen on investing in jewelry. He regularly visits our local jewelry shop's website in order to check the price. I was wondering if I could make his life easier by making a simple app which sends him regular notifications so that he can check the price. The app should be GUI and user friendly too. So yeah. Kotlin is the go to for Android development.

    The app would be open sourced, yet it wouldn't be available for download on the Google Play store, as it literally would be webscraping lmao.
